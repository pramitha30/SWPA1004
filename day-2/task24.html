Book Review: The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

This book is a timeless masterpiece that inspires readers to follow their dreams.

The storytelling is simple yet profound, making it accessible to readers of all ages.

The theme of personal legend is beautifully explored and remains the most impactful element of the book.

The beginning was a bit slow and hard to follow, but it picks up pace quickly.
By the end, I felt deeply motivated and spiritually uplifted.

Its philosophical insights and powerful symbolism are what truly make it a must-read.
