Animal Spotlight: Elephant

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence, strong social bonds, and memory. Elephants use their trunks for communication, feeding, and even as a snorkel when swimming.

26. Recipe: How to Make Lemon Rice

  1. Cook rice and let it cool.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies.
  4. Add turmeric and mix in the rice.
  5. Squeeze lemon juice and stir well.
  6. Serve with pickle or curd.
