Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Add chili powder, turmeric, salt, and garam masala. Mix well.
  5. Pour in cream and stir until the gravy becomes rich and smooth.
  6. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
